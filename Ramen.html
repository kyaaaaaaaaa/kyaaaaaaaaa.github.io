Intro to: Ramen

Here are some of my favorites, and some tips on where to go.

Description:

  • A Japanese noodle soup, with a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one of a variety of types of noodle and a selection of meats or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg. It's similar to Udon but the noodles are thinner.
  • Ramen's origins are in China, specifically with lamian, a type of Chinese noodle, and it was introduced to Japan in the late 19th century.
  • The first ramen shop was Rairaiken, which opened in Asakusa, Tokyo, in 1910.

    • Where to go:

  • My personal favorite place currently is Taishoken which is in San Francisco and San Mateo.
